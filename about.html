About PmuMentor

PmuMentor is the premier mentorship platform for Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University (PMU). Our mission is to bridge the gap between experienced professionals, faculty, and students, fostering a community where knowledge is shared, careers are launched, and academic excellence is achieved. We connect over 10,000 Mentors and Mentees across various departments, offering personalized guidance and valuable resources.

Help / FAQ

Q: How do I become a Mentor?

A: To become a Mentor, simply select the 'Mentor' role during the sign-up process. Your application will be reviewed by the PMU administration before activation.

Q: How can I schedule a session with a Mentor?

A: You can browse the Mentors page, click on a profile you're interested in, and use the 'Book a Session' button (or similar mechanism) on their profile to request a meeting.

Q: What are the 'Resources' section for?

A: The Resources section contains documents, links, and guides shared by Mentors to help Mentees with their studies, career development, and campus life.

Q: How do I report inappropriate behavior?

A: Please contact the PMU administration directly via the contact form link at the bottom of this page or email support@pmumentor.edu.sa.
